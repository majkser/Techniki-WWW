Overview

Ground-type Pokémon represent the earth and are known for their solid, defensive nature. They are often linked to the ground, soil, and tectonic power.

Strengths and Weaknesses

Strengths: Ground Pokémon are strong against Fire, Electric, Poison, Rock, and Steel types.

Weaknesses: They are vulnerable to Water, Grass, and Ice moves.

Fun Facts

  • Ground Pokémon are immune to Electric attacks, a key strength in many battles.
  • Popular Ground moves include Earthquake, one of the most powerful physical attacks.
  • Many Ground Pokémon have dual typings, like Ground/Steel (e.g., Steelix) or Ground/Flying (e.g., Gliscor).
  • Groudon, the Legendary Pokémon, is a Ground type associated with landmass expansion.
