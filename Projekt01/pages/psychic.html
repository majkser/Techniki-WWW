Overview

Psychic-type Pokémon are mysterious and represent mental power, wisdom, and supernatural abilities. They often excel in special attacks and strategy-based battles.

Strengths and Weaknesses

Strengths: Psychic Pokémon are strong against Fighting and Poison types.

Weaknesses: They are vulnerable to Bug, Ghost, and Dark types.

Fun Facts

  • Alakazam, one of the most iconic Psychic Pokémon, is renowned for its high Special Attack stat.
  • Psychic moves often have high accuracy and additional effects, like confusion.
  • Mewtwo, a Legendary Pokémon, is a pure Psychic type and is considered one of the strongest Pokémon.
  • Psychic Pokémon are often associated with mysticism and the unknown in Pokémon lore.
