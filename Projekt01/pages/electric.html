Overview

Electric-type Pokémon are known for their speed and powerful electric attacks. They often symbolize energy, innovation, and electricity's power.

Strengths and Weaknesses

Strengths: Electric Pokémon excel against Water and Flying types.

Weaknesses: They are completely ineffective against Ground Pokémon, their only true weakness.

Fun Facts

  • Pikachu, the franchise's mascot, is an Electric type.
  • Electric Pokémon often have high Speed stats, making them hard to outrun in battles.
  • Moves like Thunderbolt and Thunder are staples of Electric-type Pokémon arsenals.
  • Some Electric Pokémon have unique secondary types like Steel (e.g., Magnezone) or Flying (e.g., Zapdos).
