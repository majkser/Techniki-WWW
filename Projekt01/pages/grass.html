Overview

Grass-type Pokémon represent nature and life. They are often tied to the environment and have moves that reflect growth, healing, and the power of plants.

Strengths and Weaknesses

Strengths: Grass types are strong against Water, Ground, and Rock Pokémon.

Weaknesses: They are weak to Fire, Ice, Poison, Flying, and Bug moves.

Fun Facts

  • Bulbasaur, the first Pokémon in the Pokédex, is a Grass type.
  • Grass Pokémon often have access to moves like Sleep Powder and Leech Seed, useful for strategic battles.
  • Some Grass Pokémon, like Venusaur, gain Poison typing, while others are dual Grass/Water or Grass/Flying.
  • Grass Pokémon symbolize balance and sustainability in the Pokémon world.
